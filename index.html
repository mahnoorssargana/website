Hi, I am Mahnoor

18 y/o from Pakistan; building prespective and skills to postively impact the world.

The future belongs to the curious

I didn't have an original thought until sixth grade. And then bam : Is this all student life has to offer? The school felt like a meaningless journey, marking time without real progress.

Since then, questions have driven me: Why? How? I've been in pursuit of a deeper understanding of the world.

I've written for advocacy groups, researched how to increase female digital participation for UN through TKS, and led partnerships at Pakistan's 7th edition of a global female startup competition.

Each experience is a step toward honing my ability to create a positive impact.

Thank you for visiting!
